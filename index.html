The food processing industry in India is poised to lead the new economy in driving investments, opportunities and employment. The food processing sector plays a key role in reducing the wastage of perishable produce, enhancing shelf life and ensuring value addition at every stage of the farm-to-fork value chain. Today, the Indian food processing industry is evolving rapidly with the rise of newer product segments. This trend is an indicator of shift in consumer preferences for processed food. Further, many States which were traditionally not strong in food processing have seen rising food processing capacities and incentives. The industry is thus expected to witness an expansion by product types and geographies. The rise of the food processing industry is of enormous significance as it provides a vital link between two pillars of the economy i.e. agriculture and manufacturing. Eliminating inefficiencies and wastages, leveraging innovation and opportunities are the drivers for this sunrise industry.
